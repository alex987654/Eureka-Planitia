# Colloquial (Unofficial) Mars Surface Feature Names

Source: NASA/ESA mission archives and press materials; cross-referenced against the IAU/USGS Gazetteer of Planetary Nomenclature (https://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/). Planetocentric latitude, +East longitude.

**73 informal features** across 10 descriptor classes. These names are mission-team or popular nicknames, not IAU-approved nomenclature. Where a nickname refers to an officially named feature, the official name is given. Coordinates of rover-scale targets (rocks, outcrops) are approximate site coordinates. Values cross-referenced from the gazetteer are planetocentric; a few press-derived entries (e.g. Face on Mars) may trace to planetographic maps, a difference of at most ~0.3° in latitude.
